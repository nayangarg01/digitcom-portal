Company Profile

Dedicated to excellence in telecom infrastructure and specialized technical support since 2001.

Digitcom India Technologies is a client-centric solution company established in 2001, providing premium Telecom site support services. Our expertise spans Microwave implementations, sector addition network installations, and FM Radio Broadcast technical support & outsourcing services.

We have expanded our robust operations across multiple cities in India, ensuring that our customers remain highly satisfied with our deliverables. Our strong client services, robust support processes, and seamless operations are well appreciated by our prestigious partners who have continuously retained our services for years.

Our Core Competencies

Mobile Networking (Microwave): Complete end-to-end activity from material logistics to installation, commissioning, RFI, ATP, etc.

Mobile Networking (Sector Addition): Modifications with sector addition & preparation of complete end-to-end network activity.

VSAT Solutions: Site Survey, Site Engineering, Installation & Commissioning, Electrical & specific operations, Site logistic support, AMCs, and standard Operation & Maintenance.

FM Radio Stations: Specialized Technical support, Manpower Outsourcing, and advanced Site Engineering.
